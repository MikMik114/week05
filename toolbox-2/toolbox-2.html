Frame description

From our Corner Collection comes Quentin, a narrow frame with a lot going for it: a brow bar made from stainless steel and acetate and a keyhole bridge with style for days.

Width
Narrow

Our frames are categorized as narrow, medium, and wide to help you find your most comfortable pair. The overall width is a combination of the frame’s lens widths and bridge width measurements (in millimeters).

Measurements
49-23-142

Measurements for Warby Parker glasses can be found printed on the inside of the temple arm. Most pairs of glasses (including other brands) print their measurements in a similar place. If you already have a pair that you like, it can be helpful to compare measurements to see how a certain Warby Parker frame will fit.
If your Warby Parker frames don’t fit you perfectly, our skilled opticians would be happy to adjust your frames at select retail locations.
